What is a Filipino-style Pasta Carbonara?

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine or any chosen type of pasta
  • Water
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Chopped Mushrooms
  • Cream of Mushroom
  • All Purpose Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chopped Parsley(optional)

Pasta Carbonara Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil your chosen pasta based on the package instruction and dont forget to stir the pasta carefully to avoid compact.
  2. To prepare the sauce, start by sauteing the chopped bacon to extract the bacon fat
  3. When the bacon is already crispy, put the butter and wait for it to melt
  4. Next step is to saute the chopped mushrooms for 30 seconds or one minute
  5. After sauteing the mushrooms, pour the cream of mushroom and stir immediately upon pouring
  6. Add the All purpose cream and also stir all the ingredients together upon pouring
  7. Toss the pasta and make sure it is evenly coat with the sauce
  8. Add some Parmessan Cheese to add a little bit of saltiness
  9. (Optional) you put some chopped parsley and chopped bacon to make it presentable

Voila! we have now our Filipino-style Pasta Carbonara
